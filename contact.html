Public Outreach

Spokane Regional Emergency Communications offers a variety of public outreach programs to seniors, community group, ELS, adult service organizations and schools. We are also available to answer questions and provide information to media sources.

Look for Spokane Regional Emergency Communications at local events such as Fire Station Open Houses, Job Fairs, Valleyfest and more!
