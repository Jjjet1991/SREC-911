File an Online Report

If you are reporting an Emergency, call 911 immediately.


Using this online police report system allows you to submit a report immediately and print a copy of the police report at no cost. Please make sure to turn off your pop-up blocking software before filing the report. Start by selecting the jurisdiction in which the crime occurred below. Upon completion of this report process you will see the words: “Your online police report has been submitted”. You will also be given a temporary report case number until your report has been approved.
Click here to get a copy of a previously filed report.



If you would prefer to file your report over the phone or have any issues, please call Crime Check (509)456-2233.

Please Note:
  • Not all reports may be completed online or over the phone, some reports may require law enforcement response.
  • All cases filed using the Online Police Reporting System will be reviewed.
  • We do not process reports for crimes that occurred inside the city limits of Cheney at this time. Please call 509-535-9233 for assistance.
  • Upon review, if further investigation of your case is needed, you may be contacted.
  • Filing a false police report is a crime under RCW 9A.76.175.
  • Unfortunately due to the nature of fraud reports (including unauthorized credit card use), we are unable to process these reports online. After the card has been reported stolen online, please take a moment to call Crime Check to report the occurrences of fraud. If you could have: the Time, Date, Amount, and location of the fraudulent transactions it will speed the process along.
